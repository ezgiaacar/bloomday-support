Frequently Asked Questions

Complete tasks to earn seeds. Each completed task gives you 2 seeds, with bonus seeds if you finish during your scheduled time window or during a focus session. You can earn up to 20 seeds per day.
Complete at least one task every day to maintain your streak. If you miss a day, your streak resets — but if you have a resilience token, you can recover it. Earn tokens by reaching streak milestones like 7, 14, 30, and 100 days.
When creating a task, set a start time. Then tap the 🔔 bell icon next to the task — you'll receive a notification 5 minutes before it begins. Make sure notifications are enabled for BloomDay in your phone settings.
Plants grow when you water them and complete tasks. Each plant has a growth stage — water daily and stay consistent with your tasks to help them bloom. When a plant fully blooms, you earn bonus seeds and unlock a special moment.
Unfortunately, deleted tasks cannot be recovered. We recommend using the postpone feature (📅) instead of deleting tasks you might want to return to later.
You can manage or cancel your subscription anytime through the App Store. Go to Settings → Apple Account → Subscriptions → BloomDay, then tap Cancel Subscription. Your access continues until the end of the billing period.
Your data is tied to your BloomDay account and syncs automatically. If you log back in with the same account, your garden, tasks, and streak should be restored. If something is missing, please contact us and we'll look into it.
